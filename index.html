Kangxi period blue and white porcelain often features motifs inspired by nature, literature, and mythology...

These vases offer a compelling window into the artistry and material culture of the Qing dynasty. They demonstrate the technical skill of Jingdezhen potters, the aesthetic preferences of the Kangxi period, and the historical movement of Chinese art into Western collections. The detailed provenance information reveals the shifting value and significance attached to these objects over time.
